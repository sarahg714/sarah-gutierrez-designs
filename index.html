About Me

A Newfound Passion for Coding

I've been working in healthcare for 10 years, but it wasn't until I became a mom (to my sweet boy, Leo) that I decided it was time to make a career change. I discovered coding and it felt like a perfect fit for me. I've now found myself wanting to learn as much as I can!

My style is minimalistic and nature-inspired. I love all the beauty and color that flowers, landscapes, and oceans have to complement what you normally see in a tech atmosphere.

I'm thrilled that I gave myself the opportunity to explore and grow. I believe that anyone who is looking for a change, no matter how big or small, is allowed to go for it! You're never too old to try and start something new for yourself.

It's great to meet you. I hope we can work together to create some fun projects!
